Mac-n-cheese

Description

Dive into the ultimate comfort food with our Classic Mac and Cheese. This creamy, dreamy dish is a nostalgic favorite that combines tender elbow macaroni with a rich, velvety cheese sauce.

We start with perfectly cooked pasta, enveloped in a luscious blend of sharp cheddar, smooth mozzarella, and a touch of tangy Parmesan. The sauce is seasoned to perfection with a hint of mustard powder, garlic, and a dash of paprika for added depth. Topped with a buttery, golden crust, this mac and cheese is baked until bubbling and irresistibly gooey.

Whether you're looking for a hearty side or a satisfying main dish, this recipe promises to deliver warmth and comfort in every bite. Serve it up for a family dinner, a cozy night in, or any occasion that calls for a taste of home.

    Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Double cream
  • Paprika
  • Chilli powder
  • Onion powder
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder

    Steps

  1. Boil your macaroni, once cooked, save some of the water while draining the rest
  2. Stary off with 30g of butter in a pan.
  3. Add 2 cloves of garlic,chopped, and saute.
  4. Add 1.5 tablespoons of flour and mix it.
  5. Mix well into a consistent paste.
  6. Add 400 ml of milk and mix as you're pouring it in.
  7. Now we are to add 120ml of double cream.
  8. Next add 1 tablespoon of paprika and add 1/2 tablespoon of chilli powder
  9. 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  10. 1 tablespoon of black pepper
  11. 1 level tablespoon of salt
  12. 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  13. Mix everything into the sauce
  14. Now you can add any kind of cheese you prefer. I ususally mix mozzarella, cheddar and red leicester (50g of each)
  15. Mix that all up and keep cooking - the sauce should be quite thick and creamy
  16. Add some of the pasta water from your boiled pasta until the sauce has the consistency you want
  17. Now add your boiled pasta
  18. Mix it with the sauce
  19. Now I'm going to add a layer of mac and cheese into a tray
  20. Add some cheese on top, add a second layer and sprinkle some more cheese on top
  21. Put it in the oven, grill setting at around 200 degrees celcius for 4-5 minitues

    22. Enjoy!!
