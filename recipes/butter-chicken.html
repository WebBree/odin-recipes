Butter Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 250g of chicken
  • Ginger and garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Red chilli powder
  • Tandoori masala
  • Tumeric
  • Salt
  • Ground cumin
  • Plain yoghurt
  • Butter
  • Cooking oil
  • Small onion
  • 200g ripe tomatoes
  • Ground coriander
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Double cream
  • Dried fenugreek leaves

Steps

  1. Start of with 250g of chicken, cut into cubes
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of garlic and ginger paste mixed together.
  3. Add 1/2 tablespoon of garam masala
  4. Add 1/4 tablespoon of red chilli powder
  5. Add 1/4 tablespoon of tandoori masala
  6. Add 1/4 tablespoon of tumeric
  7. Add 1/4 tablespoon of salt
  8. Add 1/4 tablespoon of ground cumin
  9. Add 2 tablespoons of plain yoghurt
  10. Mix it all up and leave it to marinate for around 30 minutes
  11. Once it has been 30 minutes, in a pan add 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of oil
  12. Add your chicken and fry each side for 2-3 minutes
  13. Once done, remove from the pan and set aside
  14. In the same pan, add another tablespoon of butter and then 1 chopped small onion
  15. Lightly fry the onion for 2 minutes on medium heat. Their color should be light
  16. Add another 1 tablespoon of mix ginger and garlic paste. Proceed to cook and mix for 30-40 seconds
  17. Add 200g of ripe tomatoes, chopped
  18. Season with the following:
    • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 tablespoon garam masala
    • 1/4 tablespoon ground coriander
    • 1/4 tablespoon red chili powder
    • 1/4 tablespoon table salt
    • 1/4 tablespoon tumeric
  19. Mix it up and add 1/2 cup of water. Cover the pan for 2-3 minutes
  20. Once the tomatoes are well cooked, take it out of the heat and let it cool. Blend it till smooth
  21. Put it back into the pan and heat it up. Add 1/2 tablespoon of sugar and 1.5 tablespoons of lemon juice
  22. Add 70ml of double cream an cook for a minute
  23. Proceed to add the previously fried chicken and sprinkle 1/2 tablespoon of dried fenugreek leaves
  24. Cover and let it cook for 2-3 minutes
  25. Serve with some more cream and fenugreek leaves. Enjoy with some naan or rice

Enjoy!!
