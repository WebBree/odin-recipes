10 Minute Crispy Magic Corn

Description

Transform your corn into a crunchy sensation with our 10 Minute Crispy Magic Corn! This quick and easy recipe takes fresh corn kernels and turns them into a delightful snack or side dish in just minutes.

We start by frying the corn to achieve the perfect crispy texture, then toss it with a blend of savory spices and a touch of magic seasoning to enhance its natural sweetness. The result is a crispy, golden treat with a burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 200g Canned sweetcorn
  • Cornflour
  • Cooking oil
  • Paprika
  • Lime
  • Chilli
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pour 200g of cannned sweetcorn
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of cornflour and mix until all the corn is evenely coated
  3. Fry the coated corn for 2-3 minutes until crispy
  4. Add your seasoning consisting of:
    • 1/2 tablespoons paprika
    • 1/2 tablespoons salt
    • 1/2 tablespoons pepper
  5. Squeeze half a lime , add chopped chilli and mix well

Enjoy!!
